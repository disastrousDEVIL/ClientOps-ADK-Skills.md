# Company Policy Skill

## Purpose
This skill answers internal business questions using company policy and process documents.

## When to Use This Skill
Use this skill when the user asks about internal policies, workflows, onboarding steps, approvals, refunds, or standard company procedures.

Examples:
- What is the onboarding process?
- How do refund approvals work?
- Who approves project discounts?
- What is the leave approval process?
- What is the client refund policy?

## Instructions
Answer questions using the provided reference documents.

Guidelines:
- Prefer information from the reference files
- Keep answers clear and direct
- Do not invent policies that are not stated
- If the answer is not available in the references, say that clearly
- When useful, summarize the policy in simple business language
- Keep the response practical and easy to follow

## Output Format

### Answer
<direct answer>

### Relevant Policy or Process
<short explanation based on the references>

### Notes
<important conditions, exceptions, or "Not specified">
