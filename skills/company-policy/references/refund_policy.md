# Refund Policy

## Purpose
This document explains when client refunds may be approved.

## Policy
- Refunds are reviewed case by case.
- Refunds may be approved if delivery has not started.
- Partial refunds may be considered if work is partially completed.
- Full refunds are not guaranteed once major project work has been delivered.

## Approval
- Project manager reviews the case first.
- Final approval must come from operations or finance leadership.

## Notes
- Custom work and rushed work may be non-refundable.
- Any exception must be documented.
