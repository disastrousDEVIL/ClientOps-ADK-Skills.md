# Company Policy Skill Examples

## Example 1

### Input
What is the onboarding process for a new employee?

### Output

### Answer
The onboarding process starts with HR sharing joining details, followed by document submission, IT access setup, manager orientation, and tool access during the first week.

### Relevant Policy or Process
The onboarding policy says HR initiates the process, the employee submits documents, IT creates access, and the reporting manager handles orientation.

### Notes
Delays in document submission may delay access setup.


## Example 2

### Input
Can a client get a full refund after major project delivery?

### Output

### Answer
A full refund is not guaranteed after major project work has already been delivered.

### Relevant Policy or Process
The refund policy states that refunds are reviewed case by case, and full refunds are generally not guaranteed once major work has been delivered.

### Notes
Exceptions must be documented and approved by operations or finance leadership.


## Example 3

### Input
Who approves a commercial discount?

### Output

### Answer
A commercial discount requires sales lead approval.

### Relevant Policy or Process
The approval process states that commercial discounts must be approved by the sales lead.

### Notes
Final approval should be recorded in writing.
