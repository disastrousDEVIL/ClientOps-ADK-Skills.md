# Onboarding Policy

## Purpose
This document explains the onboarding process for new team members.

## Process
1. HR shares the offer confirmation and joining details.
2. The new employee submits required documents.
3. IT creates email and workspace access.
4. The reporting manager schedules an orientation call.
5. Access to tools and projects is granted during the first week.

## Notes
- Delays in document submission may delay access setup.
- Tool access depends on team and role.
