# Approval Process

## Purpose
This document explains internal approval flow for discounts, refunds, and scope exceptions.

## Rules
- Small project changes can be approved by the project manager.
- Commercial discounts require sales lead approval.
- Refund decisions require project review plus operations or finance approval.
- Scope exceptions must be documented before approval.

## Notes
- Verbal approval alone is not enough.
- Final approval should be recorded in writing.
