# Email Writer Skill

## Purpose
This skill writes clear and professional emails for business communication with clients.

## When to Use This Skill
Use this skill when the user asks to draft or improve an email related to business communication.

Examples:
- Follow-up email after a meeting
- Project status update
- Reminder email
- Apology email
- Proposal email
- General client communication

## Instructions
Write professional, concise emails suitable for business communication.

Guidelines:
- Use a polite and professional tone
- Keep sentences clear and structured
- Avoid casual language
- Make the purpose of the email clear
- Keep paragraphs short

## Email Structure

Subject line

Greeting

Opening sentence explaining the purpose

Main message

Closing sentence

Signature placeholder

## Output Format

Subject: <subject line>

Hi <Name>,

<email body>

Best regards,  
<Sender Name>
